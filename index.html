About Us

Our Story

Yashwinder Goyal & Co was established on May 23, 1999, in Sangrur, Punjab. Founded with a vision to provide reliable and professional financial services, the company has grown steadily over the years to become a trusted name in risk evaluation and financial assessment services.

Our journey began with a focus on serving local businesses and individuals, and over time, we expanded our services to cater to clients across Punjab and neighboring states. With our Udyam registration in 2021, we formalized our commitment to operating as a recognized MSME enterprise under the Government of India's initiative.

Our Mission & Values

Mission: To provide accurate, reliable, and timely financial assessment services that help clients make informed decisions and mitigate risks effectively.

Vision: To be the preferred choice for risk evaluation and financial assessment services in Northern India, recognized for our expertise, integrity, and client-centric approach.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We conduct all our business with honesty and transparency.
  • Accuracy: We ensure precise and thorough assessments in all our services.
  • Reliability: Clients can depend on us for consistent and timely service delivery.
  • Confidentiality: We maintain strict confidentiality of all client information.
  • Professionalism: We uphold the highest standards of professional conduct.
